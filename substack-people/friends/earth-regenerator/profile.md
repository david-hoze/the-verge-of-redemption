# Joe Brewer (Earth Regenerator)

**Substack:** https://earthregenerators.substack.com/

## Who He Is

Joe Brewer is co-founder of the Design School for Regenerating Earth and author of "The Design Pathway for Regenerating Earth." He focuses on bioregional regeneration - building a planetary network of bioregions to enable ecological and cultural renewal. 833 subscribers on Substack.

## Connection to David

David commented on one of Joe's pieces about bioregional learning centers (BLCs), noting the missing question: what moral architecture holds these communities together when supply chains break. Joe responded positively, noting their work with Indigenous communities and the significance of "sacred instructions" as deeper moral architecture. He affirmed the need for social scaffolding that supports BLCs through collapse.

## Key Alignment

Joe's work on bioregional communities facing collapse converges with David's argument that functioning moral traditions - not philosophical frameworks - are what hold communities together under pressure. Joe's reference to Indigenous "sacred instructions" maps directly onto David's thesis about traditions as operational governance.
