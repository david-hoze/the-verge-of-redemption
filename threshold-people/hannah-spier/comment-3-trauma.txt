The circularity in Linehan's model - "I have BPD because my parents were invalidating, and I know they were invalidating because I have BPD" - is a genuine methodological problem most clinical training breezes past. The prospective studies point matters most: follow children forward instead of asking adults to remember backward, and early trauma predicts psychopathology in general but does not specifically produce BPD. The clinical world has been running on "trauma causes borderline" without the evidence.
