The three populations you identified - the eccentric introvert, the undisciplined child, the Cluster B adult - is the clearest map I've seen of how diagnostic expansion works. Each has a real need the autism label obscures. The eccentric child needs to be left alone. The undisciplined child needs boundaries. The Cluster B adult needs accountability, not a neurotype that validates every impulse. The nonverbal child who needs lifelong care disappears into the same spectrum as the TikTok "late diagnosis."
