# How to Approach Women

Based on Day's framework (daydayday.substack.com). The good parts, distilled.

---

## The Foundation: Be Full First

Nothing works without this. A man who needs a woman repels women. A man who has built a life so complete that a woman is a bonus - that's the man she wants.

"Build your life until it doesn't need witnesses. Master something until it masters you back. Disappear into your craft until people feel your absence like a phantom limb."

The frequency to broadcast: **"I want you but I don't need you."** You can't fake it. You have to actually have a life - a body you've trained, a craft you're obsessed with, friends you'd fight for, a relationship with God. The date is dessert, not the meal.

---

## Lead Everything

Pick the place. Pick the time. Pick the wine. Don't ask where she wants to eat. "Decisiveness is foreplay. Asking too many questions is the opposite of foreplay."

She arrives. You hand her what you already poured because you made the decision. She can veto anything - but the starting position is that you have a plan and you're executing it. A man standing in the doorway of his own life asking someone else which direction to walk is a man who dies alone.

---

## The Date Architecture

**Three mechanisms: uncertainty, investment, novelty.**

- Don't tell her the full plan. She knows when and where to show up. The rest unfolds.
- Plan something that shows effort and thought - not money. She can feel the difference between a man who spent time and a man who spent cash.
- Take her somewhere she hasn't been.

**Spike, then calm.** Start with something physical - anything that raises the heart rate. Adventure, movement, surprise. Her body cannot tell the difference between fear-arousal and attraction-arousal. Then bring it down somewhere quiet. The contrast is where intimacy lives.

**Multiple locations.** Her brain timestamps each place as a separate memory. Three stops in one night feels like three dates. You've accelerated intimacy without forcing it.

---

## How to Talk to Her

Kill the small talk fast. "What do you do for work" should be illegal.

Ask her what she wanted to be when she was eight. Ask her what song she plays when nobody's watching. Ask her what she's most afraid of. Then actually listen. Ask "why" three times in a row until she's accidentally revealed something she's never told anyone - and suddenly she feels closer to you than men she's dated for months.

The voltage between laughter and truth is the most addictive frequency a man can emit. Make her spit out her drink, then make her go quiet with something so honest it lands like a fist to the sternum.

---

## Touch

Small of her back through a doorway. Her elbow when making a point. Her hand when you're both laughing. Physical comfort is a bridge you build one plank at a time. If you wait until the end of the night for first contact, it feels like a stranger grabbing her in the dark.

---

## Confidence Is Not Perfection

Food late? "Good things take time, like me." Wrong turn? "Scenic route." Spilled something? "Modern art." Confidence isn't the absence of mistakes. It's the refusal to let them shrink you. She's not looking for perfect. She's looking for unfuckwithable.

---

## Silence

If you can sit in silence with a woman you just met and neither of you reaches for a phone, you've already won something most couples don't find for months. Silence is the final exam. Most men fail it because they're terrified of what happens when they stop performing.

---

## After

Text her that night. Not three days later. Not "I had a great time" like her dentist after a cleaning. Text her something specific - something only you could text because only you were there.

"You have the worst taste in jukebox music I've ever encountered and I need to see you again immediately to discuss this further."

Specific beats vague. "The way your nose scrunches when you laugh at your own jokes before you finish telling them" gets screenshotted and sent to every friend she has.

Set up the second date within a week. Not "we should hang out sometime" - that's a ghost's handshake. "Friday. 8 PM. Wear something that makes me forget my own name."

---

## Never Stop

"Her presence is not her permanence. She is choosing you every single day and she can choose differently tomorrow and the only thing preventing that is your refusal to become boring."

The first date never ends.
