---Someone told me to kill two birds with one stone.

Went to the park.

Picked up a stone.

Two pigeons on a bench sharing a pretzel.

Couldn't do it.

They looked happy.

Went back the next day.

Same bench.

Same pigeons.

Same pretzel.

Sat down next to them.

They moved over.

We shared the pretzel.
