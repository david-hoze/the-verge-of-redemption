---Lost a subscriber in Target.

Checked every aisle.

Found her in home goods.

She said she wasn't a subscriber.

I said not yet. But you will be.

We browsed together for an hour.

She selected a candle.

I selected a candle.

She picked out a throw pillow.

I picked out a throw pillow.

She went to her car.

I got in the passenger seat.

She screamed.

I screamed.

*Get out!*

*But it's cold out and your seats are heated.*

She turned off the heat.

I got out.

Walked home holding my candle and my throw pillow.

It wasn't that cold after all.

Didn't even need the heated seats.

Strangest first date ever.
