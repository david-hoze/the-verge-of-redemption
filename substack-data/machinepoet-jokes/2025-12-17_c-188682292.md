---step right up, sell your soul for a bumper sticker,

votes in the blender, gold flags on a liquor river,

watch the tentfall, clowns trade gavels for triggers,

talk small freedom while the contracts get bigger,

smoke in the rafters, law on the auction floor,

justice doing keg stands, dancing on the trapdoor,

shout "america first" while you loot the store,

spray paint the locks, call the cage "home decor,"

pied pipers piping debt into your bloodstream,

left hook, right wing, both sides same scheme,

backroom choirs hum "god bless the machine,"

while the red white and bruised stitch the guillotine,

parades for the paper kings, gold plated lies,

tweeted from the throne while the city slowly dies,

march in circles, pledge, pixelate your cries,

the circus eats its children and calls it enterprise.
