---A subscriber told me the key to success is pulling strings.

Went to the hardware store.

Bought 40 feet of string.

Started pulling.

Nothing happened.

Bought stronger string.

Pulled harder.

Knocked a shelf over.

Manager came running.

Told him I'm trying to succeed.

Told me to get out.

Went home.

Tied string to the mailbox.

Pulled.

Mailbox came off its post.

Tied string to the neighbor's fence.

Pulled.

Fence came down.

Neighbor came running.

Told him I'm trying to succeed.

Told me he's calling the police.

Officer asked what I was doing.

Said I'm pulling strings.

Trying to succeed.

She was very understanding.

Tied a string to her bumper.

Pulled.

Now I need bail money.
