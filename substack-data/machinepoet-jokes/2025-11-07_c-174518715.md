I don't slide into DMs.

I sneak.

I peek out from behind the corner to make sure my coast is clear.

I run sideways on tiptoes hiding behind shrubs and mailboxes.

And when the DM is in sight,

I do a Triple Lindy making graceful entry with no waves or ripples.

But maybe that's just me.
