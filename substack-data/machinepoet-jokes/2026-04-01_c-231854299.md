---I heard that to succeed, you have to put yourself out there.

So I went outside.

Stood on the curb.

For nine hours.

A woman asked if I was for sale.

Said no.

She offered forty dollars.

No.

Fifty.

I went home with her.

She put me in the garden next to a ceramic gnome.

He’s been out here seven years.

Says it gets easier.

Give it time.
