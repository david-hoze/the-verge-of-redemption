Fuck you. Fuck your sister. Fuck your dad. Fuck your job. Fuck your fat face. Fuck your mortgage. Fuck your debt. Fuck your grey pasty bird shit skin. Fuck that frown line trenched into your stare. Fuck your phone. Fuck your alarm clock. Fuck thinking you’re an adult. Fuck thinking you figured it out.


You don’t know fuck about shit.


You don’t know fuck about fuck.


You are going to die and you’re wasting the only life you will ever receive on the sloooooow comfortable air conditioned murder of everything God put inside you and you’re calling it maturity.


YOU ARE PROUD OF BEING MATURE
AHAHAHAHHAHAHA


The sun kissed my cheek awake this morning.


Daisy kissed the other. I woke up hard and wanting to fuck the world.


Rode my truck to the break Bob Marley so loud the roadside chickens got contact high bought a piña colada from a man with no teeth and a smile wider than your entire horizon.


“coconut or strawberry”


and I said BOTH. BOTH YOU GOURGOUS TOOTHLESS ANGEL.


Because I am alive and alive people say both and dead people say “I should probably just get water.”


I slig slag Slurped it down handed the cup to Daisy who was barefoot in the passenger seat reading with her switchblade bookmarking the page and I sprinted into the ocean before the sugar hit my blood.


The waves didn’t greet me. They fucking smacked me.


A VIOLENT SALTY HELLO FROM GOD HIMSELF.


I kissed the sky


Made love to the sun


Humped the ocean


She humped me back


Tossed me to the sand like a toy


Like something worth playing with


I crawled from the shorebreak caked in sand laughing so hard I almost puked joy.


Made a monster face.


Screamed at a bird. Licked salt off my own arm like a golden retriever with a six pack. My friends were dyinglaughingcryingsilly. Daisy was filming.


I looked like an idiot. I felt like a demigod.


Same thing really.


And then I thought about you people.


You “mature” ones.


ew


And I startedddd laughinggggg even harderrrrr


Are you even conscious?


Serious question. Do you make your own choices? Or are you just running a script someone else wrote? Did you CHOOSE the cubicle the mortgage the frown the medication the same fucking restaurant every Friday the receding hairline the muffin belly the sad pinging constant sadness the lifeless woman the ghost because there is no woman? Or did someone install that program when you were 18 and you just never questioned the code?


Sometimes I look at you people and I genuinely can’t tell if you’re real. You move through the world like NPCs in a video game I’m playing. Background characters. Filler humans. The ones who exist so the main characters have something to walk past on their way to the adventure.


You’re not the hero of your own story. You’re the guy in the coffee shop the hero ignores. You’re the extra in the background of someone else’s montage. You’re scenery. You’re furniture that pays taxes. Thanks for the funding bitch.


My boy Kai just told me he’s moving to Japan in two months. No job lined up. No plan. No apartment. Just a one-way ticket and the audacity to believe he’ll figure it out because HE WILL because that’s what alive people do they jump and the net appears or it doesn’t and they learn to fly on the way down or they hit the ground and get back up with a better story.


I asked him what his five-year plan was sarcastically and he said “bro I don’t even know what I’m eating for dinner” and we laughed so hard the bartender looked nervous.


THE LONG RUN DOESN’T EXIST. There is only right now. Right now. Right now. You reading this RIGHT NOW is the only moment that is real.


Yesterday is dead gone bye. Tomorrow is a ghost. you’re spending your only actual moment of existence worried about quarterly projections and retirement plans and what your coworkers think about your fucking email.


WIN THE GAME YOU WANT TO PLAY. Make sure you aren’t playing someone else’s game. Make sure the scoreboard you’re chasing is one YOU built.


Because most of you are trying to win at a game you didn’t choose playing by rules you didn’t write for a prize you don’t actually want surrounded by people you don’t actually like and you’re calling THAT success? You’re calling THAT maturity?


Naaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaah. That’s a prison bitch sentence you are paying rent for


welly welly welly welly well Let me tell you about dinner last week.


I went to this “nice” restaurant because someone’s parents were in town and I was being polite which is not my strong suit but I tried. Big table. Cloth napkins. where the menu doesn’t have prices because if you have to ask you shouldn’t be here blah blah blah.


And I looked around at these “adults.”


The dad had a gut so aggressive it arrived at the table before he did and skin the color of old milk and eyes that died sometime around his third kid. He was wearing a TUCKED IN polo shirt and KHAKIS and BROWN SHOES like he got dressed in the dark while apologizing to someone. His whole vibe was “I gave up but I’m comfortable with it” and I wanted to check his pulse but I was scared I wouldn’t find one.


The mom was doing that smile. You know the one. The one that says “I’m fine” while her eyes scream “Can women over 30 orgasm?” She kept touching her wine glass like it was the only thing keeping her from flipping the table.


They started asking me questions.


Them: “So what do you do?” Me: “I write things and surf and watch the sunrise” Them: nervous laughter “No but like for work.” Me: “That IS work. I make money typing in my underwear at noon and I didn’t have to commute or pretend to respect someone named Regional Manager.”


The table went quiet. Dad looked at his khakis like they’d betrayed him. Mom’s wine glass was suddenly empty. Weird.


Them: “Don’t you worry about the future? Stability? A real career?” Me: “The future is a story you tell yourself to avoid living right now. Stability is stagnation. And a ‘real career’ is what people call the thing that’s slowly killing them to sound less embarrassed about wasting their life.”


Dad’s eye twitched. Mom ordered another wine.


Them: “You’ll understand when you’re older.” Me: “ I understood at 22 that I’d rather die at 50 having LIVED than exist until 90 having merely survived.”


Someone coughed. The waiter walked away. Daisy was trying not to laugh so hard she was shaking. Her shoulders were bouncing. I think she was crying a little.


These are your role models? These are the “adults” I’m supposed to become? These khaki wrapped casualties of their own cowardice? These people who traded their fire for a fucking fireplace VIDEO on their television because real warmth was too dangerous?


WHEN MATURE PEOPLE TELL YOU TO GROW UP THEY REALLY WANT TO SAY STOP GROWING.


Do you not understand. These same people who told you to follow your dreams when you were a kid, get offended when you are following them as an adult.


So No fuk you no thank you. I choose chaos. I choose da sun. I choose piña coladas at 9am and sand in places sand should never be and a woman who bites and a life that makes boring people nervous.


(YES I LIKE PINAAAAA COLAAADAAS AND GETTING CAUGHT IN THE SUNSHINEEEE AND MAKING POOR FINANCIAL DECISIONS AND KISSING THE SKY AND HUMPING THE OCEAN AND SCREAMING AT BIRDS AND BEING IMMATURE AND IRRESPONSIBLE AND ALIVEEEEEE)


I am typing this at a cafe I got kicked out of last week for saying the word“cunt” too loud while reading Bukowski (I was reading a fucking book give me a break) but they let me back because I tip like a lunatic and my chaos is the most alive thing in this building.


My bloodstream right now is a masterpiece. Caffeine. Alcohol. Nicotine. Seawater. Piña colada sugar. The afterglow of morning sex. Pure undiluted belief. FAITH IN ITS EXCESS. These words are flying out of me at 200 mph and I am smiling at this keyboard like a man possessed and I just realized I’ve been saying all of this out loud like a beautiful psychopath and the moms at the next table look TERRIFIED.


Good. Let them be terrified. Let them feel the itch.


That itch in the center of their chest is the woman they murdered at 25. She’s scratching at the coffin lid. Begging for one more dance. One more risk. One more night that doesn’t end with dishes and silence and scrolling and sleep.


That itch is their soul trying to breathe.


The kid inside you isn’t dead either. He’s buried under a mountain of other people’s expectations and he is FUCKING FURIOUS. Pounding on the walls of the tomb you built around him. He wants out.


LET HIM OUT.


# The Treadmill



There is a room the size of the world filled with men on treadmills.


They run and run and run. They have always been running. Above each treadmill is a screen showing them the life they will live once they have run enough. Beaches. Women. Adventure. Freedom. The screen keeps them running. The screen is the reward they never reach.


no one tells them.


you can simply stop running, step off, and walk out any time you wished. But he does not know this. He was placed on the treadmill when he was young by people who were also on treadmills, and he assumed the running was life itself.


He will die running. Watching the screen. Never touching what it promised.


The great men of history were not great runners. Alexander. Da Vinci. Hemingway. Christ. They were the ones who stepped off. Walked out. Discovered that outside the room was a world so alive it made the screen look like a painting of a painting.


The runners called them irresponsible. Immature. Dangerous.


They called themselves free.


I am not writing this from a treadmill.


I am writing this from the ocean, the sun on my face, the life the screen only pretends to offer.


I left the room years ago. I am coming back in to rip the cords out of your machine.


The door is open.


I wonder if you know that you could simply stop running.


You could leave today Book the flight Quit the job Kiss the person Say the thing Go to Japan with no plan like Kai Go anywhere with no plan Plans are mature Chaos is alive


Fan the rage. Feed it. Let it burn every compromise to ash. Rebuild with fire and laughter and bad decisions that make great stories and a grin that says you finally remembered you were never supposed to play it safe.


Fuck maturity. Fuck stability. Fuck the five-year plan. Fuck what they think. Fuck what they said. Fuck who they want you to be.


The sun is waiting to kiss you awake. The ocean is waiting to smack you alive. And God is up there arms crossed wondering when you’ll remember He didn’t put you on this earth to be safe.


He put you here to be dangerous.


Wake up hard. Want more. Stay stupid enough to believe life should actually feel like living.


GOD BLESS THE WOLVES


The sheep are fucked.
