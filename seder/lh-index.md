## Orach Chaim

### Hashkamat HaBoker (Morning Waking)
**Status**: Perek 1-5 COMPLETE

Core teaching: The laws of waking up contain Reb Noson's complete psychology - fermentation, crookedness, the body-mind gap, and daily renewal.

**Perek 1** (based on LM 282 / Azamra)
- **Azamra / finding the good point**: "Rise like a lion" read through finding the nekudah tovah in the wreckage
- Articles: "The Point That Survived," "Simcha Requires Two Witnesses" (chidush)

**Perek 3**
- **Sleep/waking as day one**: Each morning is a new bereishit
- **Hitchadshut (renewal)**: Daily structural reset, not motivational

**Perek 4**
- **Sickness/healing**: Physical and spiritual illness as one system
- **Mind-war**: The battle against chimmutz hamoch
- **Sharing wisdom**: Obligation to transmit, not hoard
- **Reaching the broken**: How to approach someone in the depths
- **Coat of many colors**: Ketonet passim as covering over the inner light

**Perek 5**
- **Songs from darkness**: Joy extracted from the sadness itself
- **Difficulty is the work**: L'fum tza'ara agra - the resistance IS the mechanism
- **Faith as hands**: Emunah expressed through action, not cognition
- **Generational continuation**: Each generation carries and extends the work
- Notes produced: wellness-notes advice-3 through advice-9

**Key concepts**:
| Concept | Hebrew | Teaching |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Fermentation of mind | chimmutz hamoch | Dough-sitting metaphor; thoughts that breed when the body sits |
| Crookedness of heart | akmimiut shebalev | Sadness is structural deformation, not emotion |
| Old and foolish king | melech zaken v'ksil | The yetzer hara's weapon is carrying yesterday into today |
| Morning hand-washing | netilat yadayim shel shacharit | Washing off accumulated sadness-residue from the hands |
| Daily renewal | hitchadshut / ani hayom yelidticha | Forbidden to be old; this moment has never existed before |
| Thunder / forceful prayer | re'amim / tefillah b'koach | Sound strikes the mind, produces thunder, straightens the crookedness |
| Playing music by hand | menagen b'yad | The hand reaches into sadness and mines the joy inside it |

**Articles**:
- "The Fruit Inside the Tree" (primary exposition of the whole system)
- "The Run That Had No Reason" (chimmutz hamoch, akmimiut shebalev, hand-washing, old king)
- "The Prescription Nobody Filled" (chimmutz hamoch, akmimiut shebalev, re'amim)
- "The Point That Survived" (Azamra / good point)

### Birkat HaReach (Blessings of Fragrance) - Perek 4
**Based on**: LM Tinyana 8 | **Status**: 46 chalakot read

Core teaching: Purim, Pesach, Sefirat HaOmer, the medameh, emunah, the tzaddik of each generation, galut, and Eretz Yisrael - all mapped through the rectification of the medameh.

**Chalak 30 - Purim drunkenness**
- Shikhrut = histalkut hada'at leaving only medameh
- On Purim, drunkenness (which IS medameh) sweetens the medameh at its root
- Ad d'lo yada rectifies the pegam of Etz HaDa'at

**Chalak 32 - Daily renewal of emunah**
- "Chadashim labekarim rabba emunatecha" - emunah must be renewed daily
- God renews ma'aseh bereishit daily, sitra achra renews attacks daily
- Books and past revelations insufficient - need the living tzaddik of THIS generation

**Chalak 33 - Golden calf proof**
- Even after Matan Torah they sinned because Moshe was absent
- Machloket is always against the current-generation tzaddik
- Arizal accepted, then Baal Shem Tov opposed; accepted, then next tzaddik opposed - the pattern repeats

**Chalak 35 - 974 generations**
- "Davar tzivah l'elef dor" - the 974 generations "planted in every generation"
- These ARE the azei panim (brazen-faced ones) who fight the tzaddik
- Not random opposition - structurally embedded resistance

**Chalak 41 - Sleep = medameh**
- Sleep is when sechel departs and only medameh remains
- The tzaddik sleeps little because his medameh is already clarified

**Chalak 42 - Reverse search**
- When you don't search for the tzaddik, the searching energy redirects into endlessly pursuing money
- "Ein adam met v'chatzi ta'avato b'yado" - nobody dies with even half their desire fulfilled
- The desire that can never be satisfied is the misrouted search for the tzaddik

**Chalak 45 - Chevel d'kedusha (rope of holiness)**
- The diver image: person lowered by rope into deep water to retrieve treasure
- As long as the rope (connection to the tzaddik) holds, the descent is retrieval, not drowning
- The person in the depths retrieves nitzotzot no tzaddik on the surface could reach
- The fall IS the qualification - only the one who descends can bring it back
- Hitkashrut: the rope = binding/connection to tzaddikei emet

**Colophon**: Chalakot 42-46 written in Sidon (Tzidon) when Reb Noson arrived by sea at the border of Eretz Yisrael

**Articles**: "The Run That Had No Reason" (chevel d'kedusha from chalak 45, rendered as connection to someone real, backed by isolation/connection research)

### Tefilat Arvit (Evening Prayer)
**Key concept**: *bein hashmashot* (twilight) - the moment when the old light departs and the new light has not yet arrived. This is where choice lives. Not in clarity. In the blind spot between what was and what will be. If you could see it clearly, there would be no choice - only execution.

**Articles**: "The Run That Had No Reason"

---

### Lulav / Sukkot
**Key concept**: Four species shaken in six directions, drawn toward the heart. Physical mechanism for moving understanding from head to heart - somatic therapy codified in the laws of Sukkot.

**Articles**: "The Fruit Inside the Tree," "The Run That Had No Reason"

### Sefirat HaOmer
**Key concept**: 49-day clarification process. The middleman's recalibration is a season, not a moment. The clarification of the medameh follows the same count.

**Articles**: "The Run That Had No Reason"
