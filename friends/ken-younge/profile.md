# Ken Younge - Friend Profile

## Who He Is

Kenneth Younge is an economist and data scientist. He has worked at or been affiliated with Purdue University, Stanford University Law School, UC Berkeley, University of Colorado, and EPFL (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology) in Lausanne, where he was most recently an Associate Professor. He holds a Ph.D.

His research is published in top economics journals: The Review of Economics and Statistics, The RAND Journal of Economics, The Journal of Economics and Management Strategy, The Strategic Management Journal, and The Journal of Economic Behavior and Organization.

He has co-founded five companies and held executive roles (CTO, CSO, Director of Development, CEO). He also co-founded a Data Science for Managers program.

Currently on sabbatical, focused on outdoor activities and public service work related to AI development and regulation.
