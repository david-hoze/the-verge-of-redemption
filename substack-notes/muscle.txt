Presence is a muscle. It atrophies.
Nobody decides to stop paying attention. You just pick up the phone one time during a silence that felt uncomfortable, and the muscle learns it doesn't have to hold anymore.
Every tradition that ever worked had a structure that forced the muscle under load - Shabbat, prayer times, fasting. Not because suffering is noble. Because the muscle only grows when it can't escape.
Now we've built a civilization that removes every reason to hold still.
And we wonder why nobody can.
