# The Web That Caught You

The grandmother. The neighbor who noticed you stopped coming to the table. The elder at the synagogue who pulled you aside and told a story that turned out to be about you. A web so dense that falling through would have required the entire structure to fail.

The entire structure failed. What replaced it - layer by layer, each substitute thinner than the last - cannot catch what the web once caught. No chatbot can track what a trained clinician feels in her body before she can name it. No clinician can replace what the grandmother knew without an intake form.
