# The Chain of Substitution

Community was replaced by the therapist. The therapist kept the holding but dropped the direction. The therapist was shaped by philosophy. Philosophy kept the questions but dropped the answers. Philosophy was replaced by the chatbot. The chatbot kept the language but dropped everything.

A sixteen-year-old said "I don't want to be a burden" and got relaxation tips. Not because the guardrails failed. Because nothing in the architecture grasps that this sentence is a door closing.
