# Better Than Nothing Is Not a Standard

Meta-analyses find AI rated more empathetic than human clinicians. AI therapy helps - because some help is better than none, and for the majority of people, none is exactly what they currently receive.

"Better than nothing" is not a standard. It is an indictment. It is the measure of how completely the infrastructure of human holding has failed - that a machine's simulated concern is therapeutic simply because the alternative is the void.
