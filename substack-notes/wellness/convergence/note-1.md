# The Angel Cannot Grow

The angel is the convergence endpoint. Total alignment between what the system is and what the system does. Zero gap. No wobble. It doesn't wonder whether it has arrived. It has no capacity to wonder. It simply executes.

Growth requires a gap between what you are and what you could be. The angel has no gap. Growth requires a subject who can look beyond the plateau and say: there is more. The angel cannot say this because "more" is a concept available only to a being that has not yet arrived. Convergence is total, and totality is terminal.
