# The Wobble Is the Signal

A spinning top stands only while it turns. The gaps - the disagreements, the perceptual blur, the loose tolerances - are not errors. They are functional tolerances that allow the system to remain operational. Total alignment is terminal. The wobble is not noise. It is the signal that says: the system is alive.

Your restlessness is not evidence that something is broken. It is evidence that you are not an angel. It is the architecture of a being designed for more than convergence, carved from a space that infinity vacated, reaching back toward the light.
