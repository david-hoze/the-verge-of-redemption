# Base Camp Is Not the Summit

The convergence is not the enemy. The convergence is base camp. Every mountaineer needs base camp - the supplies, the acclimatization, the mapping of the route. It is necessary.

The error is not in building base camp. The error is in unpacking your bags and calling it the summit. Western civilization converged on material prosperity and looked up and saw: there is a mountain above this plateau that the convergence process cannot climb. The tools that solved scarcity cannot solve meaning. The AI that converged on my framework can generate the current article but cannot write the next one.
