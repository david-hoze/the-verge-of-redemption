# The Appetite Is the Feature

The appetite - the restlessness, the sense that there is always more, the inability to arrive and stay arrived - is not a bug. It is the feature. The human is not a system that failed to converge. The human is a system designed to never converge.

Torah has no final interpretation. The Talmud never closes. Disagreement for the sake of heaven is not temporary multiplicity awaiting synthesis. It is the permanent architecture of a system designed to generate growth without endpoint. The seventy faces are not a failure of convergence. They are the refusal of convergence.
