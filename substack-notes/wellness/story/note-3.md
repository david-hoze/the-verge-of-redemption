# All Dreams Follow the Mouth

Two men had the same dream. Same interpreter. One paid, one didn't. The one who paid got a good reading and his life unfolded accordingly. The other collapsed. Same raw material. Different narration. Different fate.

Narrative therapy says you can choose your story. The Talmud says something scarier: someone else might choose it for you. The interpreter isn't a therapist. He's whoever gets to your story first - the parent, the partner, the algorithm, the voice in your head at 3 AM. Choose your interpreter carefully. The interpretation becomes the reality.
