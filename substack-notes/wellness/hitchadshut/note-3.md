# A New Version Is Still the Old One

Red sat in front of the parole board twice and performed rehabilitation. He said the right words. He showed them a changed man - a continuation of the convicted man, improved by incarceration. They denied him both times. Because a new version of the young man is still the young man. The third time he stopped performing. "That kid's long gone and this old man's all that's left." They let him go.
