# Dawn Shares Its Root With Black

In Hebrew, the word for dawn shares its root with the word for black. The first light of morning and the deepest dark use the same letters. The good point always shows up looking like failure.

That's not a defect. That's what dawn looks like before it's bright. The good you find will be contaminated. It will be surrounded by mess. If you're waiting for a clean win, you're waiting for a thing that doesn't exist.
