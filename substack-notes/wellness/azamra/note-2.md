# The Ingredient That Stinks

The ancient Temple incense had eleven ingredients. One of them - galbanum - smells terrible on its own. Any prayer that does not include the broken part is not real.

Most self-help says: focus on your strengths. Ignore your weaknesses. The opposite is true. The weakness belongs in the recipe. Not because you celebrate it - but because excluding it makes the whole thing incomplete. The honest life includes the galbanum.
