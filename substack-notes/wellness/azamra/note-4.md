# The Weight Is Real and Incomplete

When the weight hits - the failures, the distance, the "how did I get here" - do not argue with it. Don't tell yourself it's not real. It is real.

But it is not the whole picture. The mistake is thinking you have two options: accept the weight as the truth, or deny the weight with positive thinking. Both are wrong. The weight is real and incomplete. Those two things are not a contradiction.
