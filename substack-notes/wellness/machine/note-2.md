# The Machine Eats Its Own Preconditions

Nobody sat down and decided to destroy the family, hollow out the community, or replace the folk song with the jingle. It happened one reasonable decision at a time, each making perfect sense in isolation, each removing something that didn't seem load-bearing until the ceiling came down.

The meaning crisis is not a failure of the system. It is the system working perfectly. A civilization organized around productiveness will systematically eliminate everything that is not productive. Not as a conspiracy. As a logic.
