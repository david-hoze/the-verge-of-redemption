# The Simpleton Became King

The sophisticate travels the world, learns every craft, becomes a doctor, a jeweler, a philosopher. Never happy. Never satisfied. Always finding new flaws. The simpleton gets married and fixes shoes. His wife brings bread and water and he says: what tasty meat and wine.

We live in a time of infinite possibilities. We can live anywhere, work in anything, learn whatever we want. If we don't like someone, we can just go. The mind keeps nagging: I should probably leave. But the simpleton became king. That's a promise. Believing it is probably the hardest thing in life.
