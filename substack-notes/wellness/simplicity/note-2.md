# Give a Real Chance to Where You Are

We just have to believe that where we are is where we're supposed to be. That there's a reason we married this person, met these friends, live in this building. Not that we can never leave. That we have to give a real chance to where we are. Really make it through.

Why should a simpleton become a king? It doesn't make any sense. And that's the choice we are given. Each and every one of us. Each and every day.
