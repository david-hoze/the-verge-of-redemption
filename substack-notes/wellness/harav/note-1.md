# Duck Your Head

A man survived a shipwreck with nothing but a plank. His whole technique: every wave that came, he ducked his head. He didn't fight the wave. He didn't try to climb over it. He just lowered his head and let it pass. The big troubles in your life are waves. You don't beat them by standing tall - you beat them by not giving them a target. Lower your head. Do the next small thing. The wave passes. You're still floating.
