# 100% of Your 50%

You're responsible for half the relationship. But your responsibility for that half is total - unconditional, with no dependence on whether the other person is holding up their end. "I'll do my part when they do theirs" sounds reasonable. It's actually 0% of your 50%. The only version that works is: I own my half completely, regardless of what's happening on their side. That's not unfair. That's the only math that doesn't deadlock.
