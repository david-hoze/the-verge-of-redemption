# Conflict Is a Diamond

Every fight hurts. But the pain is a signal that you stepped on something valuable. Couples who never fight aren't peaceful. They've stopped walking. They're standing still, avoiding every spot where a diamond might be buried, keeping the surface smooth and the depths untouched. The fight isn't the problem. The fight is the map. It shows you exactly where the buried thing is. The question isn't how to stop fighting. It's whether you're willing to pick up what you stepped on.
