# The Socks on the Floor

She's not upset about socks. Here's what happens: socks on the floor means he's messy. Messy means irresponsible. Irresponsible means he's not a real partner. Not a partner means she's carrying everything alone. Alone means she made a mistake. A two-second mess activates a five-layer wound. And she can't explain it because by the time she's angry, she's angry about being alone - not about socks. He hears "pick up your socks" and thinks she's controlling. She means: I need evidence that I'm not alone in this house.
