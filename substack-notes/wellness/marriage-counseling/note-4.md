# Don't Apologize Yet

Saying sorry too fast shuts down the other person's pain before they've finished feeling it. It looks generous. It's actually selfish - you're apologizing to make yourself comfortable, to end the discomfort of watching someone hurt. Let them have the stage. Let the pain be fully expressed before you respond to it. A premature apology says: your pain is making me uncomfortable, so I'm going to resolve it now. What they need is the opposite - the experience of being fully heard before anything gets resolved.
