# The Arrogance Is the Wound

The person who can't stop proving how smart they are, how successful, how important - that person isn't confident. They're protecting something fragile underneath. The grandiosity is a cast over a fracture. You don't treat a fracture by attacking the cast. You treat it by acknowledging what broke. The loudest person in the room is almost never the strongest. They're the one most afraid of being seen as weak. Genuine confidence doesn't announce itself. It doesn't need to.
