# The Child Who Kept Getting Sick

The doctors couldn't find a cause. The child was sick constantly - no explanation, no diagnosis, no treatment that worked. A therapist discovered: every time the child got sick, the parents stopped fighting. They cooperated. They were gentle with each other. They functioned as a team. The child had found the only lever that turned his parents into partners. He wasn't choosing to be sick. His body was solving a problem the adults couldn't solve. The child was the symptom. The marriage was the disease.
