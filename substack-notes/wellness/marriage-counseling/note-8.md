# What Triggers the Wound Also Heals It

The closeness that reopens your oldest pain is the same closeness that can repair it. Your partner's behavior triggers something from long before they arrived - a wound from childhood, from the first people who were supposed to love you. But the fact that this person's closeness can reach that wound means this person's closeness can also reach the place where repair happens. You don't need to understand the original trauma. You don't need to excavate the past. The corrective experience happens in the present relationship, not in the past one.
