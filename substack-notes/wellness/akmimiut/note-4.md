# You Cannot Skip to Sukkot

The calendar is a curriculum. Rosh Hashanah is the thunder. The ten days are the straightening. Yom Kippur is the completion. Sukkot is the joy that arrives because the structure can now produce what straight structures produce.

You cannot grab the harvest without the plowing. The person who wants happiness without the straightening work is the person who wants to start the year in the fourth month.
