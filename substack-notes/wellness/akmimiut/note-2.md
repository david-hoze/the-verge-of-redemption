# The Joy Inside the Sadness

The happiness industry says: process your sadness so you can move past it to joy. The teaching says the opposite. The joy is not past the sadness. The joy is inside the sadness. If you erase the sadness, you erase the joy with it. They are structurally coupled.

The hand that plays music is the hand that reaches into the darkness and sorts. Not every dark feeling is the same. Inside the sadness there are deposits - memories, longings, connections, unfinished love. The musician's hand finds them. The running person never does.
