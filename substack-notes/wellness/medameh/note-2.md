# The Resemblance Is the Weapon

The corrupted imagination doesn't show you something completely false. It shows you something that looks like something true. It makes the cult leader look like the teacher, the conspiracy look like the pattern. And the person who has been captured does not experience themselves as deceived. They experience themselves as seeing more clearly than everyone else. Because the faculty of vision, when corrupted, doesn't make you blind. It makes you see vividly - the wrong things.
