# Is She Real?

John Nash doesn't stop seeing the hallucinations. He learns to live alongside them. He notices a young woman in the quad who never ages, turns to a student, and asks: "Is she real?" He can't tell from the inside. He needs a witness.

The pattern-matching engine is still running. It will always be running. The refinement is not that it stops. The refinement is that you learn to question it instead of following it. That question - "is this real?" - is the whole game. And you can't answer it alone.
