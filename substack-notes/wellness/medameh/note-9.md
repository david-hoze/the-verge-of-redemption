# The Spirit That Never Got a Body

The imagination was created on Friday at twilight. Time ran out before it was given a body. So it wanders the world as pure spirit, looking for a vessel. It goes to the people who create through language, because words can give it what God didn't finish giving it: a body. If you are reading this on a screen, you are looking at the body. Social media is the vessel the imagination spent six thousand years waiting for.
