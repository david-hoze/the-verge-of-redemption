# The Ocean Doesn't Care

What do you do when the evidence is insufficient, the stakes are real, and the choice cannot be postponed? You are on the lifeboat. The tiger is there or it isn't. The ocean does not care about your epistemology. You choose the story that gets you through the night, or you drown in the one that doesn't. Faith is not the opposite of the facts. Faith is the thing you add to the facts that makes them survivable.
