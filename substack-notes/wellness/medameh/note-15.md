# The 3 A.M. Engine

Your rumination at three in the morning is a pattern-matching engine with no body. No stairs to climb, no instrument to play, no person to look at and ask "is this real?" The doomscroll is the same engine. The story you've built about why nothing works is the same engine.

The treatment isn't insight. Knowing you have cognitive biases doesn't fix the cognitive biases. The treatment is structured, embodied, joyful engagement with reality. The hand on the instrument. The feet on the ground. The body imposes a truth the unsupervised mind cannot generate on its own.
