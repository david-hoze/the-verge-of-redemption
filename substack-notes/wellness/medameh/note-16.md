# The Spirit of Foolishness

You don't sin unless a spirit of foolishness enters you. Not weakness. Not bad character. Temporary insanity. Something enters, does its work, leaves. You're left holding the wreckage asking "how could I have done that?" The honest answer: you didn't. Something else was driving.

Every wrong turn you ever took, you took while the faculty you'd use to check was the one that was compromised. The treatment is not "try harder." It is "don't put yourself in the situation where the spirit can enter."
