# Every Level Has Its Own Oz

The delusions of the entry-level employee are not the delusions of the executive. The executive's are better tailored. Higher resolution. More plausible. The person who conquered wanting-a-bigger-house now faces wanting-respect. The person who conquered wanting-respect now faces wanting-legacy. The imagination upgrades. The yellow brick road never ends. There is always another Emerald City, and the wizard behind the curtain always has the same amount of nothing in his hand.
