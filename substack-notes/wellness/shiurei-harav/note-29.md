# The Partner You Didn't Want

The force that opposes you is not trying to destroy you. It is trying to develop you. Resistance is not an obstacle to growth - it is the mechanism of growth. A muscle that meets no resistance doesn't strengthen. A person who meets no opposition doesn't develop character. Your worst moments - the ones you're ashamed of, the ones that exposed you - built more in you than your best moments did. The thing you're fighting is the only reason you're getting stronger. If it disappeared tomorrow, you'd stop growing by Thursday.
