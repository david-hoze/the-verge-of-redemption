# The Rhythm Nobody Keeps

You get close. Then you lose it. Then you get close again. This is not failure. This is the design. Growth happens in the oscillation, not in the arrival. The people who think they've permanently arrived are the ones who stopped growing. The ones who feel lost every other week are the ones who are actually moving. The cycle of clarity and confusion is not a bug in the system - it is the system. A heart that beats is a heart that contracts and expands. A heart that only expands is not alive. It's inflated.
