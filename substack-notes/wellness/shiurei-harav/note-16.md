# Today You Were Born

Yesterday doesn't exist. You didn't learn yesterday, didn't show up yesterday, didn't do the thing you promised yesterday - none of that matters today. Today is a new creation. You are not the sum of your failures. You are the person waking up right now. The voice that says "you already blew it" is trying to keep you in bed. It wins every time you let yesterday's collapse become today's excuse. Even in the worst situation - the one where everything has been taken - someone is still breathing, still thinking: maybe I'll still build something. Today you were born. Start.
