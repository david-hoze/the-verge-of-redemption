# One Outburst

One explosion of anger can destroy a hundred things that were quietly working. Relationships, trust, your child's sense of safety, your reputation, years of built goodwill - a single uncontrolled moment can tear through all of it. The damage isn't proportional to the duration. A five-second eruption can undo five years of patience. The people around you don't remember the five years. They remember the five seconds.
