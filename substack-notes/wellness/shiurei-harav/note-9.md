# The Son Knows

If the father truly loves what he does - genuinely, not performatively - the son will too. But if the father goes through the motions, even perfectly, the son won't want any of it. Because the son is the interior of the father. He doesn't hear the words. He reads the source code. You can say all the right things and do all the right things and the child will still walk away if the love isn't real. Children don't inherit your practices. They inherit your actual desires. Whatever you truly love at the deepest level - that's what they'll carry. Everything else falls off.
