# The Monkey

A monkey watched a rich man check gold coins with his teeth. Day after day, the monkey watched. One day the monkey grabbed the coins and bit down. Choked. Died. The monkey was a perfect imitator. Flawless technique. Dead. That's what imitation without understanding produces. You watched someone successful and copied their moves. You have the morning routine, the journal, the vocabulary, the aesthetic. But you don't know why they did any of it. The moves without the meaning will choke you the same way the coins choked the monkey.
