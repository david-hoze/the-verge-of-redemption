# If It's Easy

If it came easy, it came from the wrong place. The things that change you resist you. The book that transforms your life is the one you put down six times before finishing. The relationship that matters is the one that makes you want to run. The practice that works is the one that bores you to tears on Tuesday. If your growth feels like a high, check the source. Highs are designed to feel good. Growth is designed to produce something. They feel nothing alike.
