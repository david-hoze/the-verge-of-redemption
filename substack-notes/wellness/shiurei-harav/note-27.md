# The Mirror You Can't Hold

You cannot see your own face without a mirror. This is not a metaphor. It is a structural limit. The thing you most need to understand about yourself is the thing you are structurally unable to perceive. Your blind spot isn't where you're ignorant. It's where you're confident. You need someone who will tell you what you look like from the outside, and you need to not fire them when they do. The people who surround themselves only with agreement aren't protected. They're blind.
