# The One Who Couldn't Go

The person who went to the retreat came back with pride. The person who wanted to go but couldn't - who sat at home with a broken heart, watching everyone else post pictures - that person got more from not going than the other got from being there. Because the point was never the location. The point was the broken heart. The one who arrived with pride missed it. The one who stayed behind with longing found it. If you're jealous of someone else's spiritual experience, you might already have the thing they went looking for.
