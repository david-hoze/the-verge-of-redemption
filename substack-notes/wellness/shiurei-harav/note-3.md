# Wait Five Minutes

When she says "wait five minutes" - wait five minutes. That's it. That's the whole protocol. But if you say "what did I do?" - now it's twenty minutes. And if you say "but really, I genuinely don't know what I did" - now it's an hour. Every sentence you add to prove your innocence doubles the sentence. She didn't ask you to understand. She asked you to wait. The ability to sit in five minutes of silence without defending yourself is the single most underrated skill in any relationship.
