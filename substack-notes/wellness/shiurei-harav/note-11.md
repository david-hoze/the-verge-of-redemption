# The Hormones Run Out

You had a breakthrough. You felt something. You were certain this time was different. That feeling was real - but it was fuel that burns in hours, not years. Without structure underneath it, emotion is just a chemical event. The people who actually change their lives aren't the ones who felt the most. They're the ones who built something on the morning after the feeling left. Inspiration is a match. Discipline is a furnace. The match is spectacular. The furnace is ugly. The furnace heats the house.
