# She Is Not Your Servant

Your wife is not someone who exists to maintain your comfort. She is not the person who cooks, cleans, and manages the logistics of your ambition. She is the intelligence the house runs on. When you treat her as support staff for your life, you are announcing that you have no idea what you married. Every good thing in your house - the stability, the children's character, the fact that any of it works at all - runs through her. Honor that or watch it leave. Not her. The good thing. It'll leave before she does.
