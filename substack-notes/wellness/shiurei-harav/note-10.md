# The Blackest Point

The part of your eye that sees is the pupil - the tiny black dot. The place that looks like it has nothing is the place where everything enters. The most important things are always the most hidden. The skill nobody notices. The relationship nobody photographs. The quiet person in the room who holds everything together. You keep looking for meaning in the bright, visible, impressive parts of your life. It's not there. It's in the black dot. The place that looks empty is where the seeing happens.
