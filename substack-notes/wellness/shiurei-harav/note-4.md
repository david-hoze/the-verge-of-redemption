# You Think You Deserve Something

Anger is always a symptom of entitlement. Always. A person who truly believes he deserves nothing - nothing from his wife, nothing from his children, nothing from the world - cannot get angry. The anger tells you: somewhere in you there's a belief that this situation should be different because you're important enough to deserve better. You're not. Everything you have is a gift you didn't earn. The food on your table, the person sitting across from it, the fact that they're still there. Once you see that, the anger has nothing to grip.
