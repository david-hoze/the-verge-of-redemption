# In a Circle, No One Is First

Study makes you proud of what you know. Prayer makes you proud of how devoted you are. But physical movement in a group - dancing, working alongside others - collapses the hierarchy. In a circle there is no first and no last. You can't tell who's leading. The moment you think you're at the front, you've already misunderstood the shape. If your growth practice doesn't include something that makes you look foolish alongside other people, it's probably feeding the thing it's supposed to be fighting.
