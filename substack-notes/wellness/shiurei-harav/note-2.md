# A Cup of Tea

The biggest fight in your house can be resolved with a cup of tea and a piece of cake. Not a conversation about feelings. Not a couples retreat. Not a book. Sit down, pour the tea, eat together, say nothing important. That's it. The entire machinery of destruction in your relationship runs on one fuel: absence. The enemy of your marriage isn't conflict. It's the fact that you won't sit down for five minutes. Every force in the universe conspires to prevent you from doing this one small thing. Do it anyway.
