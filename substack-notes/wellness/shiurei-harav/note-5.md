# The Astronaut

People who transform their lives have incredible momentum. They fly past everything. New beliefs, new habits, new identity - they reach the moon. But the moon is desolate. And when they come home, they come home with arrogance: "Who are you to tell me anything? I was on the moon." So the wife leaves. And they end up alone. Go live on the moon by yourself. Transformation without integration destroys the people around you. The journey up is only half the work. The other half is coming back slow enough to sit at the table with people who stayed on the ground.
