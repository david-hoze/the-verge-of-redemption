# The Righteous Fool

You can be perfectly sincere and perfectly stupid at the same time. Good intentions don't compensate for the absence of wisdom. Some people are deeply kind and deeply wrong. They mean well and they damage everything they touch - not despite the sincerity but alongside it. Sincerity without intelligence is a car with no steering. The engine runs great. The direction is random. Don't trust yourself just because your heart is in the right place. Your heart being in the right place is the beginning, not the end. After the heart, you still need a brain.
