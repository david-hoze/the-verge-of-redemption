# Don't Wait

A man was dying on the road. A traveler stopped. He said: let me organize my bags first, let me sort what I'm carrying. By the time he finished organizing, the man was dead. The traveler spent the rest of his life paying for that pause. When someone needs you, you don't get to optimize first. You don't get to find the best approach, the perfect words, the right moment. The right moment was when they asked. Everything after that is a decision to let them wait while you get comfortable.
