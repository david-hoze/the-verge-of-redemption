# The Days It Counts Most

The practice doesn't need your enthusiasm. It needs your attendance. The days you show up feeling nothing - no motivation, no insight, no connection, no reason to be there - are the days the practice is doing its deepest work. Enthusiasm is the experience of the practice. Discipline is the structure. The structure holds when the experience doesn't. A bridge that only works on sunny days is not a bridge. The days you feel nothing and show up anyway are the days you're building something that will hold your weight when the weather turns.
