# Slowly, Slowly

Your worst enemy is more patient than you. He doesn't rush. He doesn't attack head-on. He follows every piece of advice you follow - persistence, consistency, never giving up. He waits at 20, at 30, at 40, at 50. He doesn't despair. One moment of weakness, one second of believing you've arrived, one flash of "I've got this figured out" - and he's in. The thing working against you is not stronger than you. It's more patient. It will wait your entire life for one opening. The only defense is to never believe you're done.
