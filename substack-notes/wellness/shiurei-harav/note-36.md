# The Ash Over the Ember

A preacher tried to ignite an audience and nothing happened. Someone asked why. He said: there was no ember in the coals - no amount of blowing helps without a spark. But he was wrong. Every person has an ember. It's not dead. It's buried under ash - habits, disappointments, years of being told their fire doesn't matter. Your job isn't to bring the fire. It's to clear what's covering the fire that's already there.
