# The Bread and the Car

Put a piece of bread in a car. The car won't move. Put it in an airplane. The airplane won't move. But a person eats a piece of bread and he sings, dances, jumps, builds, fights, loves, creates children, writes books. There is no greater miracle than this. You ate toast this morning and turned it into a decision to keep going. The bread didn't change. You changed it. Stop looking for the extraordinary. You are the extraordinary. The conversion of wheat into hope is the most astonishing thing happening on this planet and it happens every breakfast.
