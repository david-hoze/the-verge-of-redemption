# Come Home With Something Small

Every time you walk through the door, bring something. Not expensive. A word. A question about their day. A piece of chocolate. A compliment you thought of on the way home. The size of the gift is irrelevant. What matters is the evidence: you thought of them while you were away. That's what they actually need. Not the object. The proof that they exist in your mind when they're not in front of you.
