# Every Day Is the First Day

Treat the person you live with as if you're meeting them for the first time. The way you spoke to them when you were trying to win them - that's how you should speak to them for the rest of your life. Not because they need to be won again. Because the respect you showed when you were trying IS the respect. The version of you that showed up on that first day wasn't a performance. It was an intention. Keep the intention.
