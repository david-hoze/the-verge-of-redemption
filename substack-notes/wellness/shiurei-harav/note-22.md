# Is This What I Expected

A teacher looked at his students and said: is this what I expected from you? That you'd rent cars and drive to me? That you'd jump on my tables? I wanted you to have an intellect that hasn't existed for generations. The students thought showing up was the point. The teacher wanted them to become something. Showing up is not becoming. Enthusiasm is not growth. The teacher doesn't want your attendance. He wants your transformation. And every hour you spend performing devotion is an hour you didn't spend doing the actual work.
