# A Quarter Page

You want to be a genius in two years. When you're not, you'll despair and quit. Here's the actual method: a quarter page. Read it twenty times. Not five. Twenty. Every pass reveals something the last one hid. People fail at learning not because they're slow but because they're in a hurry. They cover ground instead of occupying it. The person who reads one page twenty times owns it. The person who reads twenty pages once rents all of them and owns none. Slow down. You're not behind. You're trying to go too fast.
