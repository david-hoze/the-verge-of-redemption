# The Moon Got Smaller

She said something true and was told: shrink. That's the pattern. The person who names the real problem gets punished for naming it. Not because they were wrong. Because the system isn't ready. So she got smaller. And the getting smaller is what eventually made her able to hold more than anyone else in the room. The people who were never diminished never developed the capacity. You were made smaller and you think it was injustice. It might have been. It also might have been the only way to build a vessel big enough to hold what's coming.
