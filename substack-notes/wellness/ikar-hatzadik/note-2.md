# You Know More Than You Live

There's a fire in you that won't settle - it comes out as impatience, isolation, a quiet anger at yourself for not living up to what you understand. So you pull away from people, convinced they're the problem. They're not. The gap between what you know and what you actually do is what's burning you. The fix isn't more insight. It's Tuesday night with someone ordinary, doing something that doesn't require you to be the smartest person in the room.
