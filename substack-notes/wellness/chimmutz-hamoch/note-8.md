# Choice Lives in the Dark

Choice exists precisely because you are standing in the dark. If you could see it clearly, there would be no choice - only execution. The cruelest paradox: the smarter you are, the harder this is. The person who has built their identity around understanding - asking that person to set down the tool of understanding is asking them to abandon what feels like their best self. Not ignorance. The costly act of a person who genuinely understands choosing to move anyway - because the understanding will never finish arriving.
