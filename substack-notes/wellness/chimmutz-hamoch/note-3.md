# Thoughts With No Floor

Some thoughts have a floor. Your child is struggling - what do you do? You said something cruel - how do you repair it? These deserve your full attention. They have a bottom. Then there is another kind. Am I wasting my life? Does any of this matter? Is it too late? These thoughts have no floor. You can think them for twenty years in a therapist's chair and never land. The void does not get smaller when you examine it. It gets larger.
