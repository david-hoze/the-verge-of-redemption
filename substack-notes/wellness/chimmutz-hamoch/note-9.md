# The Reason Arrives After the Legs

Walking and jogging produced the largest effect size of any intervention tested for depression - larger than yoga, larger than strength training, larger than every cognitive technique. The body was never waiting for a reason. The body was waiting to be released from the chair. And if the body won't move - if the depression is so heavy the legs won't cooperate - the deepest work is not the walking. It is the refusing to leave. The run doesn't need legs. It needs the part of you that hasn't quit.
