# The Fermentation

When dough sits idle, it rises and sours. When the mind sits idle, it does the same thing. Comparisons. Regrets. Fantasies about outcomes that will never arrive. The seated mind doesn't just produce bad thoughts - it produces thoughts that breed. One worry produces a comparison. The comparison produces a regret. The regret produces a fantasy about a life you didn't live. The multiplication is the mechanism, not the content.
