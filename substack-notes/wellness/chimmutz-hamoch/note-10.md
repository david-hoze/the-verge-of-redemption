# The Golden Calf Was Not a Failure of Intelligence

The moment you stop searching for the real thing and accept a replacement, the corruption takes over. The golden calf was built by people who had just experienced the most profound clarity of their lives and stopped looking for the source of that clarity. They substituted something they could build themselves, and it consumed them. This is what happens when the self-help book replaces the teacher, when the framework replaces the relationship, when the technique replaces the daily practice that has no name and no glamour and works.
