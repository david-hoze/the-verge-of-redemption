# The Scum That Rises

Before you started working on yourself, everything looked fine. The water was clear. But it wasn't clean - the dirt was just mixed in so well you couldn't see it. Now that you've started doing the work, everything ugly is floating to the surface. The thoughts, the patterns, the parts of you that make you flinch. That's not a sign you're getting worse. That's the purification working. The mess was always there. You just couldn't see it until the heat turned on.
