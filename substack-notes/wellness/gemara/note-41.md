# Three Questions

If I'm not for myself, who will be? If I'm only for myself, what am I? If not now, when? The first kills passivity. The second kills selfishness. The third kills delay. You need all three in order, or the whole thing collapses - self-care without service is narcissism, service without self-care is martyrdom, and either one without urgency is just a plan you never execute.
