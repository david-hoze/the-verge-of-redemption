# Don't Say What Won't Be Heard

It's not just wrong to stay silent when you should speak. It's also wrong to speak when you won't be heard. Saying the right thing at the wrong time, to the wrong person, in the wrong state - that's not honesty. That's self-indulgence wearing honesty's clothes. Before you tell someone the truth, ask: can they actually receive this right now? If not, your silence is the more loving act.
