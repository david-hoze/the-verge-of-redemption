# Pray for the Sin, Not the Sinner

Someone was being tormented and wished for the people hurting him to disappear. His wife said: don't wish the people gone - wish what's broken in them gone. He did. They changed. The difference between "I want you gone" and "I want what's wrong in you gone" is the difference between destruction and repair. One removes the person. The other leaves room for who they could become.
