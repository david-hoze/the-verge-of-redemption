# Your Worst Moments Are Building Materials

There's a level of change where your past isn't just forgiven - it's converted. The failures, the betrayals, the things you can't take back don't get erased. They get reclassified. What was a debt becomes a deposit. Not because someone let you off the hook, but because when you change deeply enough out of love rather than fear, the person you become could only have been built from exactly those mistakes. Your worst material becomes your best resume.
