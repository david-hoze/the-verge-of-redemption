# Sincerity Beats Correctness

A wrong thing done with your whole heart outranks a right thing done on autopilot. That doesn't mean wrong is right - it means the engine matters more than the direction. A person heading the wrong way with real fire can be turned around. A person heading the right way with no fire is already lost. Passion aimed wrong is a steering problem. No passion at all is an engine problem, and engine problems are harder to fix.
