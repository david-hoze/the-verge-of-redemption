# You Weren't Stupid

Nobody does the thing they know will destroy them while thinking clearly. Every relapse, every self-sabotage, every "how could I have done that" - you weren't making a decision. Something else was driving. A temporary insanity moved in, did its work, and left you holding the wreckage. That's not an excuse. It's a diagnosis. And it changes the question from "what's wrong with me" to "what got in."
