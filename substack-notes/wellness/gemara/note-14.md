# If You've Never Been Tempted

The people who mock the fallen are always one step from falling themselves. Two of the wisest men who ever lived laughed at how easy it should be to resist temptation. Both were immediately tested so hard they barely survived - and only made it because of something outside their own strength. If you've never been tempted, you've never been tested. And if you look down on someone who lost the fight, you're announcing you haven't been in one.
