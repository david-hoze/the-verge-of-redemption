# The Liar's Tax

The punishment for lying isn't getting caught. It's that eventually, even when you tell the truth, nobody believes you. You don't lose credibility in one shot - you lose it slowly, as people stop being able to tell the difference. By the time you need someone to really believe you, you've already spent that currency. The tax on dishonesty isn't paid when you lie. It's paid when you're finally honest and it doesn't matter.
