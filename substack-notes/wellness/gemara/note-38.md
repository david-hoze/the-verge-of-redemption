# I Learned Most From My Students

The greatest teacher said: I learned more from my students than from anyone who taught me. Not humility - mechanics. Teaching forces you to say what you think you know, and the moment you say it out loud, you find out whether you actually know it. Your student's confusion is a mirror for your own. The questions you can't answer are the edges of your understanding. You thought you were giving. You were receiving the whole time.
