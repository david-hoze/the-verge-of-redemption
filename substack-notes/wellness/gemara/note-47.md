# What Destroyed Everything

The first time everything was destroyed, it was because of the worst things imaginable - violence, corruption, depravity. The second time, it was because of something smaller: people hating each other for no good reason. The small poison did more damage than the large one. Baseless contempt - the snide comment, the cold shoulder, the decision that someone doesn't deserve your respect - took down what even the worst crimes couldn't. The thing that finally breaks everything isn't the big evil. It's the small, petty kind.
