# You Weren't There

A scholar dreamed of a man history called wicked. In the dream, the wicked man knew more than the scholar. The scholar was stunned. The man said: if you'd lived in my time, you would have done worse than I did. That's the end of every sentence that starts with "I would never." You don't know what you would do. You've never been tested by their test. The confidence that you'd handle it better is the exact confidence that guarantees you wouldn't.
