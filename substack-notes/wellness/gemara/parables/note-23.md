# Four Hundred Times

A teacher repeated every lesson four hundred times for one student. One day the student couldn't focus - he knew the teacher had somewhere to be. So the teacher repeated it four hundred more times. That's not patience. That's a completely different definition of what teaching is. The lesson wasn't the content. The lesson was: I'm not leaving. You're worth eight hundred repetitions. Most people quit on you after the third time you don't get it. The ones who stay past four hundred are the ones who actually change your life.
