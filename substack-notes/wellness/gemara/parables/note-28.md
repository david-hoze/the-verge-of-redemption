# Miracles Don't Happen Every Hour

Two friends got drunk at dinner. One killed the other. Prayed him back to life. Next year, same invitation. The answer: miracles don't happen every hour. He didn't say "you killed me." He didn't say "I'm angry." He said: I survived once and I'm not betting on twice. That's the sharpest boundary anyone ever drew. No blame, no drama, no punishment. Just: I know what you're capable of when you lose control, and I love you too much to let you find out if it happens again.
