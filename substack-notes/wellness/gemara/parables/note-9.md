# You Already Knew

Before you were born, you knew everything. All of it - every answer, every connection, every truth you've been scratching at your whole life. Then someone slapped you and you forgot. That's why real learning feels like remembering. That flash of recognition when something lands - it's not new. You've been here before. You're not building from nothing. You're excavating. The knowledge is already in you. The work is getting back to it.
