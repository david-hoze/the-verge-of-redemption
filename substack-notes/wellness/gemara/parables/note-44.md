# The Thirty-Six

Thirty-six people hold up the entire world. They don't know they're doing it. Nobody knows who they are. They're not famous. They're not powerful. They're not trying to save anything. They're just living with enough integrity that the whole structure doesn't collapse. You've met some of them. The person who does the right thing when nobody's watching. The neighbor who checks on people without being asked. The one who holds it together so others can fall apart. They don't know they're load-bearing. That's what makes them load-bearing.
