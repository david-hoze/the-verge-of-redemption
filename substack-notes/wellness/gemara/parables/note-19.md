# The Short Road

A wise man asked a child at a crossroads which way to go. The child said: that road is short but long. This road is long but short. The wise man took the short one. He got stuck. Every shortcut you've ever taken in your relationships, your health, your work - that's the short road that's long. The boring, repetitive, unglamorous path is the long road that's short. The child knew. The wise man didn't. Wisdom isn't knowing the answer. It's listening to the person who does.
