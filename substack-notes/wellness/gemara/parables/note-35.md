# You Bound One

Seven sons were killed in front of their mother, one by one, for refusing to kneel. Each one chose death. The youngest held the longest conversation with the man who would kill him. When it was done, the mother said: tell Abraham - you bound one son on an altar. I bound seven. Then she jumped. That's not martyrdom as the world understands it. That's a woman filing a complaint with the founder of her faith while standing in the wreckage. She didn't ask for comfort. She didn't ask for meaning. She said: I outperformed you. Count them.
