# As Hungry as You Entered

A fox found a vineyard behind a fence with a hole just big enough to squeeze through - if he starved himself for three days. He did. Got in. Ate everything. Then couldn't get out. Starved himself for three more days and squeezed back through. Stood on the other side exactly as hungry as he started. That's every promotion, every acquisition, every "once I get this I'll be satisfied." You starve to get in. You gorge. You starve to get out. And you're standing on the same side of the fence with nothing to show for it except the memory of grapes.
