# The Escape Route

The wisest man alive had two people he wanted to protect from death. He figured out the one place death couldn't reach and sent them there. The next day, death was smiling. That's where I was supposed to collect them. Your running is the delivery mechanism. The city you flee to, the relationship you escape into, the new identity you build to avoid the old one - those are exactly where the thing you're running from was scheduled to meet you. The wise man's intelligence didn't save them. It completed the route.
