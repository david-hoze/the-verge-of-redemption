# The Eye and the Dust

The most powerful man in the world brought a human eye to the wise men. They put it on a scale. Nothing could outweigh it. Gold, silver, jewels - the eye wanted more. Then they covered it with a pinch of dust. Instantly it weighed nothing. That's desire. While you're alive it outweighs everything. The moment it ends - and it will end - it weighs nothing at all. You're chasing something that won't survive the dirt they throw on it. Knowing that won't stop the chase. But it might stop you from burning down everything else to feed it.
