# The Right Diagnosis, the Wrong Cure

Someone sees you struggling and offers a way out. Leave, they say. Start over. New city, new job, new life. The logic is clean. The problem is real. But the solution kills you faster than the problem does. A fish in dangerous water is still a fish in water. A fish on dry land is just dead. When things get hard, the people who love you least will offer you the most logical escape. The ones who love you most will say: stay in the water. We'll figure it out here.
