# The Jewels You Were Holding

A woman's children died. She didn't tell her husband right away. When he came home she asked him a question: if someone lent you something precious and now they want it back, would you return it? Of course, he said. She showed him. That's the reframe that doesn't make it hurt less but makes it survivable. Everything you love was lent to you. You knew that. You just didn't think the loan would be called in today. The grief is real. The ownership was always an illusion.
