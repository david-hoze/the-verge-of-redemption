# You Can't Free Yourself

A healer visited a sick man and healed him. Then the healer got sick. He couldn't heal himself. Someone had to come for him. That's the rule: a prisoner cannot free himself from prison. You can see everyone else's problem clearly. You can give perfect advice. You can be exactly right about what's wrong. And you still can't do it for yourself. Not because you're weak. Because the lock is on the outside. Stop pretending you don't need someone to come get you. You do. Everyone does.
