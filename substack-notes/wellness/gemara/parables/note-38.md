# The Founder Couldn't Follow

The man who started everything traveled forward in time to see what his work had become. He sat in the back row. He couldn't follow a word. He was confused, disoriented, probably embarrassed. Then a student asked the teacher: where did you learn this? The teacher said: it was given to the founder. And the founder was comforted. You planted something and it grew into something you don't recognize. That's not failure. That's what growing looks like from the root's perspective. You don't need to understand what you started. You just needed to start it.
