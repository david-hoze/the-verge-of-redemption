# The Hole in the Rock

A man couldn't read at forty. He saw water dripping on a stone. The stone had a hole worn through it. Not from force. From repetition. He went and started learning. Twenty-four thousand students later, he was the greatest teacher of his generation. You're not too late. You're not too old. You're not too far behind. The water didn't smash the rock. It just didn't stop.
