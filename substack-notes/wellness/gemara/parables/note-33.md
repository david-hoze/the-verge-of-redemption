# Can a Son Be Ashamed

A prince ran away from home. Did things he can't undo. The king sent a messenger: come back. The son said: I can't face you. The king sent back one line: can a son be ashamed to return to his father? That's the only thing standing between you and going home - the conviction that what you did makes you unwelcome. It doesn't. The door isn't locked. It was never locked. The shame that keeps you away is louder than the voice calling you back, but it's lying. You're allowed to go home. You were always allowed to go home.
