# Steal from a Thief

A man's entire investment turned to vinegar. His friends said: check your behavior. He said: the only thing I did was take back what someone stole from me first. They said: steal from a thief and you taste the taste of stealing. Your justification doesn't change what the act does to you. "He started it" doesn't protect your wine. "She did it first" doesn't keep your investment from souring. The universe doesn't grade on a curve. The thing you did while feeling justified still lands in your body the same way it would if you had no excuse at all.
