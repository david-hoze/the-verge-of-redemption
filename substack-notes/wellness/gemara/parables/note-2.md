# Under My Own Seat

A man on a boat starts drilling a hole under his seat. The other passengers scream at him. He says: relax, it's under my seat. The water doesn't care whose seat it's under. That's every person who says "it's my life, my choice" while living in a family, a community, a shared anything. Your choices flood the boat. The hull is shared. There is no such thing as a private hole in a public vessel.
