# The One Who Was Right

The moon said to the sun's maker: two kings can't share one crown. She was right. So she was told: diminish yourself. Then the maker said something no one expected: bring me an offering of atonement, because I diminished her. The one who spoke the truth got punished for it. And the one who punished her admitted the punishment was wrong. That's what happens when you're right in a system that isn't ready to hear it. You shrink. And somewhere, quietly, the system knows it owes you something it will never fully pay.
