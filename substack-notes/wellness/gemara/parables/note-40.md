# Into the Furnace

A man gave charity to the same family for years. Anonymously. One day they almost caught him. Rather than be identified, he threw himself into a furnace. He'd rather burn than let the person he helped feel indebted. That's the version of generosity nobody teaches. Not the kind that gets a building named after you. The kind that would destroy itself before it destroyed the other person's dignity. The gift isn't the money. The gift is that they never have to feel grateful to your face.
