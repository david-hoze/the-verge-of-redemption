# Scream for Help

The most disciplined man in town got hit with a temptation he couldn't handle. He didn't meditate. He didn't breathe through it. He didn't journal. He screamed "FIRE!" at the top of his lungs until the neighbors came running. He was saved by public humiliation, not by willpower. That's the actual protocol. When the thing is stronger than you - and it will be - the move is not to fight it quietly. The move is to make so much noise that someone shows up before you finish climbing the ladder.
