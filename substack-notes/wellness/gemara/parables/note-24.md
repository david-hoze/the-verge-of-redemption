# The Ugly Vessel

A princess mocked a wise man for being ugly. He asked her: does your father keep his wine in gold vessels? She went home and poured the wine into gold. It turned to vinegar. The best things in your life are probably in ugly containers. The marriage that doesn't photograph well. The job that doesn't impress at parties. The friend who isn't fun but shows up at 3 AM. You keep pouring things into prettier vessels and wondering why they turn sour. The container isn't the problem. Your need for the container to look good is the problem.
