# The Torch I Can't See

A blind man walks through the streets at night carrying a lit torch. Someone asks: why do you need a torch? You can't see. He says: the torch isn't for me. It's so you can see me, and help me avoid the holes in the road. That's what vulnerability actually looks like. Not weakness. Strategy. You show people where you are so they can help you avoid what you can't see. The torch is the admission: I need you. Most people would rather fall into the hole than carry the thing that says they can't see it coming.
