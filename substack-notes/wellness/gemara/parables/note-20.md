# The Proof Nobody Wanted

Sages stood on the ruins of the holiest place on earth and wept. One of them laughed. They asked him how he could laugh. He said: the prophecy that said this would be destroyed came true to the letter. That means the prophecy that says it will be rebuilt is just as reliable. The destruction is the proof. Your worst outcome - the thing that actually happened, the thing you're standing in right now - is not the end of the story. It's evidence that the system that predicted it also predicted what comes after. The ruins are the receipt.
