# A Single Moment

A man saved an entire people by winning an argument. His reward was a death sentence - because you don't beat a tyrant and walk away. In his final moments, he joined the people he'd saved. Someone watching wept and said: there are those who acquire their world in a single moment. You think you need a lifetime to become who you're supposed to be. You don't. You need one moment where the cost is clear and you pay it anyway. Most people never get that moment. Some people get it at the very end. It's enough.
