# Is It Burning or Glowing

A man looked at a palace and couldn't tell if it was on fire or lit from within. Both readings survived. Nobody resolved it. That's the honest position on almost everything that matters. Your life looks like a disaster or a process depending on when you check. The marriage is falling apart or being rebuilt. The career is collapsing or composting. You want someone to tell you which one it is. Nobody can. The ambiguity is not a failure of perception. It's the actual state of things.
