# The Inner Chamber

In the outer rooms, everything looks fine. Celebration, gladness, composure. In the inner room, weeping. That's the structure. The public face and the private one. You think you're the only one holding it together on the outside while falling apart on the inside. You're not. Even the system that runs the universe has an outer chamber and an inner chamber. The gladness is real. The weeping is also real. They coexist. The mistake is thinking you have to pick one.
