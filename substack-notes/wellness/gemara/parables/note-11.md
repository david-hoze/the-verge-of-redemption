# The Man Who Laughed at Trees

A man saw someone planting a tree that wouldn't bear fruit for seventy years. He laughed. Who plants something they'll never eat from? Then he fell asleep for seventy years. Woke up. The tree was loaded with fruit. The planter's grandson was eating from it. The man tried to go home. Nobody recognized him. He begged to die. That's the punishment for refusing to build what you won't finish. You don't get to skip the long game. You get to be the person who wakes up in a world built by people who didn't skip it, with nothing to show and no one who knows your name.
