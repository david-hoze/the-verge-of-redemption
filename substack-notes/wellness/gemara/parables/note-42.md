# One Woman's Testimony

Everyone tried to argue the case. The greatest advocates in history, one after another. Brilliant arguments. Airtight logic. Nothing worked. Then one woman spoke. She didn't argue. She told her story - what it cost her personally to do the right thing when no one was watching. That's what moved the unmovable. Not the argument. The testimony. You've been trying to convince people with logic and wondering why it bounces off. Stop arguing. Tell them what it cost you. That's the only thing that gets through walls that logic can't.
