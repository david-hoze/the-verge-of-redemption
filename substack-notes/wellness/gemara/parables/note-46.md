# The Ring

The wisest man alive captured something dangerous and put it to work building something holy. When the work was done, the dangerous thing asked for one small favor: just hold my ring for a moment. He handed it over. The thing hurled him four hundred miles and took his throne. That's how you lose everything. Not to the big threat. To the small request that seems harmless. Just one drink. Just one conversation. Just let me hold your boundaries for a second. The thing that destroys you will never ask for the kingdom. It'll ask for the ring.
