# Take Out My Eye

A king offered a man anything he wanted. One condition: his neighbor gets double. The man thought about it. He said: take out one of my eyes. That's envy. Not wanting what they have. Wanting them not to have it - so badly you'll pay with your own body. You've done a version of this. Turned down something good because someone else would benefit more. Sabotaged something that was working because the wrong person was also winning. Envy doesn't want the prize. It wants the other person's loss. And it will eat you alive to get it.
