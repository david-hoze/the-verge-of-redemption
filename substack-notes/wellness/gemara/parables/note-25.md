# The Clear World

A man's son died and came back. He said: I saw an upside-down world. The people on top here were on the bottom there. The people nobody notices here were honored there. His father said: you saw a clear world. That's the reframe. This one is upside-down. The one where status, money, and visibility determine worth - that's the distortion. The world where the quiet people who do the work are honored and the loud ones who take the credit are invisible - that's the clear one. You already suspect this. You just keep playing by the upside-down rules because everyone else is.
