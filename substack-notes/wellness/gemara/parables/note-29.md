# He Jumped the Wall

A man about to die asked his escort for a knife - just so he wouldn't be scared on the way. Then he asked to see where he was going. When they lifted him up to look over the wall, he jumped in and refused to come out. The system let it stand but took the knife back. One person may cheat the system. The system itself cannot be abolished. That's grace - not the cancellation of the rules, but the one exception that proves they're still running. You don't get to end death. But once in a while, someone negotiates a side door.
