# The One Who Said Nothing

Three men were asked what to do about a group of people someone powerful wanted destroyed. The first said: destroy them. The second said nothing. The third walked out. The first was killed. The third's grandchildren sat in the highest court in the land. The second - the one who said nothing - got a lifetime of suffering. Not for what he did. For what he didn't say. Silence in the presence of cruelty isn't neutrality. It's a vote. And the bill comes due whether you meant to cast it or not.
