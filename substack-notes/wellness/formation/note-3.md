# You Cannot Form a Person Without Failing in Front of Them

A tradition that only shows its teachers being right produces students who think learning means being right. A tradition that shows its teachers struggling, correcting, being corrected - that produces students who think learning means wrestling.

AI cannot fail. It can simulate uncertainty, but the student knows the difference. The AI has no stakes. It cannot be changed by the encounter. Formation requires bidirectional vulnerability. That is not a feature you can add to a chatbot. It is the thing that makes formation formation.
