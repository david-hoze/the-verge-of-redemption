# The Danger Is AI as Education

AI is the best tool ever invented for filling informational gaps. It is infinitely patient. It explains the same concept fourteen different ways without judgment. None of this is the problem.

The danger is not AI in education. The danger is AI as education. When the chatbot replaces the chevrutah, when personalized learning means a child never has to navigate the discomfort of learning alongside a peer who thinks differently - the muscle atrophies before it ever develops. The adults who lost it had it once. The children who never develop it won't know what they're missing.
