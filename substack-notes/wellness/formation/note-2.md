# Twenty-Four Objections

Rabbi Yochanan's study partner died. The rabbis sent a replacement - a brilliant scholar. Every time Yochanan made a point, the new partner said: "There is a source that supports your view." Yochanan went mad.

Not because the replacement was stupid. Because every response was confirmation. The old partner raised twenty-four objections and forced twenty-four answers. Understanding only emerges when you are forced to defend what you think against someone who genuinely might be right. An AI tutor does what the replacement did. It supports. It confirms. It never raises objections from a position of genuine uncertainty, because it has no position.
