# The Body That Blocks

Daniel thought he was learning karate. He was wrong. He was learning to be the kind of person who could do karate. Miyagi didn't give him information about blocking. He gave him a body that blocks.

That difference - between forming a person and filling a person with information - is the difference every AI tutoring company is ignoring at scale. The tradition's definition of understanding is not what the student can reproduce. It is whether the student can produce something the teacher did not give them.
