# Sharper Is Not Closer

You sharpen your position. Your opponent sharpens theirs. Both of you concede real points. Both refine. The exchange feels like everything intellectual work should be. Four rounds in, you're more sophisticated than when you started. You haven't moved an inch.

The experience of getting sharper is indistinguishable from the experience of getting closer. But they are not the same thing. You can sharpen a blade forever without cutting through a wall that blades cannot cut.
