# The Pivot After the Concession

Each time a point is lost, the question is reframed to keep the approach alive. The reframe is not dishonest. It's the natural next question from within the paradigm. But it shifts the conversation from "what is the answer?" to "can my method get closer to the answer?" - which assumes the method is the right type.

This is how every unwinnable debate sustains itself. Not through confusion. Through excellence. The better the interlocutor, the more convincing the progress, the harder it is to see you're running optimized strategies inside an unwinnable game.
