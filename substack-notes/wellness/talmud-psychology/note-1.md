# The Tempter and the Prosecutor Are the Same Guy

The voice that whispers "go ahead" is the same voice that screams "look what you did." One entity wearing two masks. The craving pushes you toward the behavior. The shame after pushes you back toward the craving.

Modern addiction psychology discovered this as the shame-relapse cycle. The Talmud had the structural diagnosis in the 3rd century. And it said something the moderns didn't: it's one entity. You're not managing a conflict between desire and conscience. You're recognizing a single adversary's strategy.
