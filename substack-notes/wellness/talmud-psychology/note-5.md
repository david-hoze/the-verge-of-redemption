# Go Back to the Cave

A man spent thirteen years doing the deepest spiritual work of his life. When he emerged, everything he looked at burst into flames. A voice from heaven told him: go back. You're not ready.

The wellness industry would celebrate this man. The tradition sent him back. Your enlightenment without gentleness is incomplete. The work of transformation is only half the work. The other half is coming back soft enough to live among people who haven't done it.
