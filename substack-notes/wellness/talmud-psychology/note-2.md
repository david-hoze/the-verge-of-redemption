# The Chickens Stopped Laying

They once captured the drive that caused all the trouble. Locked it up. Within three days, no fresh egg could be found in all of Israel. The drive that wrecked lives and the drive that created life were the same drive.

Freud called this sublimation. Jung called it shadow integration. The Talmud said: we blinded it. We didn't fix it. We didn't heal it. We limited it. Containment - not transformation - is what actually works with the parts of yourself that are dangerous and generative at the same time.
