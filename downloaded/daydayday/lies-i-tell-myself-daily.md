# LIES I TELL MYSELF DAILY

**Subtitle:** (and why they work better than your truth)
**Author:** Day
**Date:** April 7, 2026
**Source:** https://daydayday.substack.com/p/lies-i-tell-myself-daily

---

"I'LL ONLY HAVE ONE DRINK TONIGHT"

I say as I stare into my green eyes in the bathroom mirror. God laughs somewhere. I wink. A smirk widens into a grin because it's not true. It's never been true.

We both know it, the devil looking over my shoulder, the angel, and the green eyed son of a bitch looking back at me all fucking know.

We know that one drink is going to become seven. Seven is going to become me barefoot on somebody's patio at 1 AM delivering a sermon about who fucking knows what to a probably terrified couple from Portland who just wanted a quiet vacation night out.

Bowie is playing. Daisy is behind me in a black dress that should require a fucking permit, doing something to her eyelashes with a tiny weapon, hair half up, half down, half gold, half sun, smelling like vanilla and the rest of my stupid beautiful life.

I grab her by the waist and spin her into me and we're dancing in the bathroom, her heels clicking on tile, my faded red and black linen shirt half buttoned, silver chain swinging, and she's laughing into my neck and the Uber is four minutes away and I haven't even found my rings yet and I don't care because THIS shit right here is the treasure I have stolen and most men don't even know they're losing it.

The Uber honks. We spill out the door like two people fleeing a murder. She looks incredible. I look like trouble.

I lie to myself every single Day. Deliberately. The way bad priests pray. The way addicts relapse. The way a fat fuck says no to the donut. The way a man who just told his reflection he'd only have one drink puts on his rings knowing dammmmmmn well the night is going to eat him alive and he's going to let it because he's hungry too.

Every "realistic" "grounded" "practical" dead eyed cubicle dweller will never understand: reality is not what happens to you. Reality is what you fucking build with the lies you're brave enough to believe before anyone gives you permission.

Here are the lies. And why every one of them is more useful than your truth.

---

**"I'LL ONLY HAVE ONE DRINK TONIGHT"**

Never been true. Not once. But this lie is about permission to start. "I'll just write one paragraph" produced every essay I've published. I'll go to the gym for few minutes. I'll catch one wave, "I'll just look at flights" is how I ended up in Hawaii eating rotisserie chicken for eleven days building a writing money printing business out of rage, Mezcal, and espresso.

Every great decision of my life started with a small beautiful lie that got me off the couch and once momentum grabbed me by the throat I was already gone. Stop committing to the whole thing. Lie about the size of it. Let momentum do what momentum does.

**"I'M NOT SCARED"**

I am scared constantly. Scared when I quit the jail. Scared the first month when nobody subscribed and my bank account looked like a suicide note written in red ink. But I said it every morning in the mirror like a hex on my own cowardice and something happens when you lie about fear long enough, the lie grows a skeleton, it hardens into something load bearing. The fear is still in the basement. I built a castle over it and I don't go downstairs anymore.

**"I'M THE BEST WRITER OF MY GENERATION"**

Am I? By what metric? Who gives a shit. I write like I believe it and that's why the writing hits harder than if I wrote like a man who thought he was average. I'm not telling you to be delusional. I'm telling you to be delusional ON PURPOSE. A crazy person doesn't know he's lying. A dangerous person knows exactly what he's doing. Every man I admire believed something about himself that wasn't true YET and held on with both fists until reality bent around it. Your beliefs are not descriptions of the world. They are instructions for it. They want me to tone it down but God turned me up and I don't argue with God.

**"I DON'T NEED ANYONE'S APPROVAL"**

When I only need God's approval number one and the people I loves second and nobody fucking else's it frees me to write the shit that actually makes people stay. The less you chase approval the faster it chases you. People can smell desperation through a screen. They can smell freedom too. Freedom smells better. Always has.

**"TODAY IS THE MOST IMPORTANT DAY OF MY LIFE"**

It's a Tuesday. Surf, write, steak, Daisy, cigarette on the Balcony. Nothing special. But if I believe today is the Day then I write like it's the Day and I grab her like it's the last time and I live like there's a camera crew from heaven documenting the whole thing for a highlight reel God is cutting together for my arrival. The man who treats every single Day like it's disposable has a disposable life.

**"I'M GOING TO LIVE FOREVER"**

This lie makes me surf waves that could kill me and grab Daisy in the kitchen like I just got back from a war I started and book flights with money I haven't made yet. The recklessness is not the side effect. The recklessness is the whole point. HAVE SOME FAITH WOULDYAAAAAA.

**"I KNOW WHAT I'M DOING"**

I have no fucking clue. I dropped a career with a pension and a union to write on the internet from a rock in the middle of the Pacific Ocean and some mornings I wake up and the first thought is what the fuck did you do and then I walk to the mirror and say I know exactly what I'm doing and the system resets and I sit down and I write like a man with a plan even though the plan is just don't stop don't apologize don't go back to the cage.

**"ONE MORE"**

One more wave. One more paragraph. One more round. One more drink (see lie number one, the snake eats its tail, the cycle never dies). Smallest lie. Most lethal. Everything good in this world is hiding behind the wall most men hit and quit at. One more punches through.

**"I'M FINE"**

The universal lie of men everywhere. I say it when the writing won't come and I miss Home at 2 AM for reasons I can't explain to an English girl (it's the place that almost killed me and I carry it like a bullet the surgeons decided to leave in because digging it out would cause more damage than just letting it sit there and ache). The lie buys time to stop the bleeding before anyone sees. By the time it expires I usually AM fine because the lie gave me the runway to land the plane.

**"THIS IS ALL GOING TO WORK OUT"**

The final boss. I whisper it in the dark when Daisy's asleep and the doubts pour in like tide water through the cracks. I can't know if it's true. But everything good in my life exists because I believed a lie long enough for it to become real. Hawaii was a lie until I bought the ticket. Took the ride. The career was a lie until strangers started paying me to bleed on a page. Daisy was a lie until she was standing in arrivals with ruined mascara and I realized the lie had quietly become the truest thing I'd ever told myself.

Your reality is a construction site. The blueprints are the lies you choose before anyone gives you permission. Build to your liking.

> Matthew 17:20: "For truly, I say to you, if you have faith like a grain of mustard seed, you will say to this mountain, 'Move from here to there,' and it will move, and nothing will be impossible for you."
>
> - JESUS

Fuck what is. I'm building what could be. With my hands and my lies and my girl and God and a stomach full of mezcal and a head full of fire and the refusal to ever, for one single second, be small.

The Uber pulled up four hours ago. We're still out. It was not one drink. It was never going to be one drink. Daisy's shoes are off. My shirt is half unbuttoned. I'm telling a stranger at the bar that his wife deserves to be kissed like she's leaving tomorrow and he's looking at me like I might be insane and he might be right and I don't care because this is what it looks like when a man builds his own reality and lives inside it like a king in a castle made of beautiful, useful, beautiful lies.

Lie daily. Lie in the direction of the man you're becoming.

Then prove every last one right until the liars become the prophets and the realists become the headstones.

GOD BLESS THE WOLVES.

God Help The Rest.
