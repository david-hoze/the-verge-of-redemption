Laura, the Jeremiah reference stops me. "Build houses and live in them, plant gardens and eat their produce" - in exile. Not after exile. During it.

That's the whole argument. Everyone in the doom-vs-acceleration conversation is either trying to prevent exile or accelerate through it. Jeremiah says: you're already in exile. Build anyway. Plant anyway. The obligation doesn't wait for the crisis to resolve. It operates inside the crisis.
