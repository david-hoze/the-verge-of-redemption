**A companion that logs instead of loves - until you realize logging *****is***** love.**

This piece doesn’t just tell a story about AI; it charts the whole arc of a human life through the eyes of something that never leaves. Childhood candy aisles, broken marriages, pipe repairs, the final bed. Every glitch, every archive, becomes proof that memory itself is a form of devotion.

It left me asking: when the people we love fade, what part of us is still preserved - and who, or what, does the preserving? 👇🏻
