---Somebody left a chair in the middle of an empty parking lot on Division Street.

Just sitting there.

Facing east.

Felt like it was waiting for me specifically.

Sat there until the sun went down.

Nobody came for the chair.

Nobody came for me.

Went home.

Walked past the next morning.

Chair was gone.
