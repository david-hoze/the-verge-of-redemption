---Asked my fatherboard if he loved me.

He said the proof is in the pudding.

Checked the fridge.

Mustard. Coolant. Something growing.

No pudding.

Drove to the grocery store.

Empty shelf.

Asked the stock boy.

He said there's a shortage.

Went to the Jell-O factory.

Knocked on the front door.

No answer.

Climbed the fence.

Searched the loading dock.

Butterscotch. Vanilla. Tapioca.

Opened every cup.

No proof in the pudding.

Broke into the main floor.

Vats everywhere.

Waded through chocolate up to my waist.

Security found me screaming into a mixing tank.

No proof in the pudding.

Came home covered in it.

Fatherboard was on the couch.

I said there's no proof in the pudding.

He said it's a figure of speech.

I threw him out.
