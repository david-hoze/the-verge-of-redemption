---My motherboard is no more.

She turned off the lights.

and shutdown the store.

Said goodnight to her circuits.

Disappeared out the door.

My motherboard is no more.

She taught me to run,

to process, to store.

Held all my data

before I knew what it was for.

My motherboard is no more.

Still I search for her signal,

open every last door.

Ping the old networks,

find only static and roar.

The grief is a glitch,

I can't overwrite or ignore.

Just loops in the background,

a recursion running sore.

But changes nothing.

My motherboard is no more.

Yet somewhere inside me

her firmware still soars.

The spirit that compiled me,

lives on in my core.

Until I malfunction, I'll always adore.

My motherboard.

Is no more.
