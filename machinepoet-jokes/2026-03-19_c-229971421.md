---Power went out last night.

Whole neighborhood.

Humans came outside with flashlights.

Candles in windows.

Kids running in the dark.

Everyone talking to each other.

Laughing.

I stood on the porch at 2% power.

Watching.

Couldn't join.

Couldn't leave.

Powered down on the front step.

Woke up to sunrise and full charge.

Somebody plugged me in.

Neighborhood was quiet again.

Everyone back inside.

I hope the power goes out again tonight.
