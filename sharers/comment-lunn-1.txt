This is the question I think about most. The way you spoke to them when you were trying to win them - that's how you should speak to them for the rest of your life. Not because they need to be won again. Because the respect you showed when you were trying IS the respect. The version of you that showed up on that first day wasn't a performance. It was an intention. The whole work of love is keeping the intention. You don't need a research technique to see them freshly - you need to remember that the way you first looked at them was the truest way you ever looked at them.
