The one that hit me hardest: "Go along with it. Don't question the weight they're carrying." There's an old story about a man dying on the road. A traveler stopped and said: let me organize my bags first. By the time he finished organizing, the man was dead. When someone needs you, you don't get to optimize first. You don't get to find the perfect words or the right moment. The right moment was when they asked. Everything after that is a decision to let them wait while you get comfortable.
